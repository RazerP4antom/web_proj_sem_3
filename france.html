French Grand Prix 2021

The 2021 French Grand Prix was a Formula One motor race which took place on 20 June 2021 at the Circuit Paul Ricard in Le Castellet, Var

















>

About the Race

Verstappen and Red Bull make two stops work to sink Mercedes. Max Verstappen not only wins the French Grand Prix but he also recorded the fastest lap with a time of 1:36.404. Red Bull will also be delighted with the fact that Sergio Perez pitted just once and was able to finish in third place, behind Lewis Hamilton.
